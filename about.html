ABOUT US

Idyllic magazine, a student-run digital magazine, started by Sanskriti jain to establish a magazine that gives young people a platform to express themselves through any form of art. This is a space for the youth to express themselves. 

Our aim is to give teenagers and kids a platform where they can fully express themselves without any judgements. We can only achieve our aim through your active participation. 

So, how can you be a part of this?

You just have to send us a piece of your art, which can be anything from paintings to poetries to essays and personal stories. What makes you, YOU? 

Send your Submissions at Idyllicmagazine@gmail.com 

We hope you participate in our small mission to make a big Impact.
