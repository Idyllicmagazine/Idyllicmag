She

By Sanskriti




She thought she didn’t belong there,

Although she wanted to be

But she wasn’t sure if others wanted her to be,

She was confused if this was her home,

She was confused if she belonged to this world,

She thought her home is far away from here,

But little did she know that she belonged there.



She never tried to be someone else,

She never wished for love like others,

She was happy with herself,

She never felt the need of someone,

But others made her feel miserable.

She was hoping for a bright morning

But she never knew how tough this is going to be

When she realized the importance of being free.

Others never let her be.



by Sanskriti
